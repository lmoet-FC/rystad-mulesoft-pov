Challenge 1 — System Consolidation

From fragmented tools to a single source of truth

Today, Rystad Energy runs its commercial operations across SugarCRM, Outlook, Freshdesk, Excel, and Chargebee. Each team has found ways to make it work — but the friction is real. Client Services often works almost entirely outside the CRM, which means deals, renewals, and client interactions live in inboxes and spreadsheets rather than in a shared system.

What this looks like today:

  • Client interactions logged manually — or not at all
  • Renewal risk buried in email threads, not visible to leadership
  • Duplicated data entry across CRM, support desk, and billing
  • No single view of a customer across commercial and service teams

What MuleSoft makes possible:

  • SugarCRM data migrated cleanly into Salesforce with full history preserved
  • Freshdesk cases automatically linked to the right Salesforce account
  • A single, complete customer record accessible to every team
  • New integrations built in days, not months — using reusable API building blocks

Challenge 2 — Finance Visibility

Live financial data where your team actually works

Right now, there's no live connection between Chargebee — where subscriptions are managed — and Salesforce, where your commercial team tracks relationships and renewals. The result? ARR figures depend on manual Excel exports, payment status is always slightly out of date, and finance and sales are never quite working from the same numbers.

The status quo costs you:

  • Hours spent every week reconciling Chargebee exports with CRM records
  • Renewal risk invisible until someone manually checks Chargebee
  • Delayed revenue recognition from data that's days or weeks stale
  • Finance and sales teams operating on different versions of the truth

With MuleSoft connecting Chargebee to Revenue Cloud:

  • Payment status and subscription changes flow into Salesforce in real time
  • ARR, MRR, and churn figures are always current — no reconciliation needed
  • Upcoming renewals trigger automatic alerts to the right account owner
  • Finance and sales work from a single, authoritative revenue picture
